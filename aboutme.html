Hobbies

From a young age I had two main interests, this was technology and sports. I ended up gravitating towards sports as I got older and into college age which led me to major in Exercise Science as my first degree. I ended up obtaining 2 trainer certifications in health and fitness but after pursuing success this field for a few years I realized I didn't like certain aspects of it and decided I would much rather keep sports and exercise a hobby. This is when I went back to school to pursue a second degree in IT.

I do not regret pursuing the exercise science field as it gave me useful leadership and life skills. I currently use that knowledge I learned to pursue my main hobby of competitive bodybuilding and weight lifting. I currently compete in 2-3 bodybuilding or powerlifting competions per year and run in endurance or OCR races with friends in between. I also play soccer or any other sport I get an opportunity for throughout the year. During the summer I enjoy beach vacations and just being outdoors as much as I can. I do not often play video games, but a new hobby I have developed now that I have a knowledge base of a few programming languages is creating them. I am currently working on multiple games that I had dreamed up when I was younger and feeling the excitement of coming up with new ideas and putting them into action.
